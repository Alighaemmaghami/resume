About Me

It served as my second opportunity to contribute as a researcher and data scientist, allowing me to thrive in an advanced environment.

My enthusiasm for the realm of data is boundless, as I am fascinated by the exploration of patterns and trends within vast datasets that are accessible to us all. I am open to embracing any opportunity that can enhance my experience of this dynamic field including an internship or a full-time job as I am graduating in 6 months.
